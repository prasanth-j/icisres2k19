ABOUT ICISRES'19

               International Conference on Information Sciences & Renewable Energy Sources (ICISRES - 2k19) aspires to bring together researchers, industrial experts, academicians and students to exchange information related to their research work in the listed topics and discuss about the challenges faced in their work and the solutions arrived. Further the authors can meet their peers in their field in international level and collaborate in research related activities. The ideas presented in the conference in different fields may be an eye opener for the participants to move towards product development and patent filing.
